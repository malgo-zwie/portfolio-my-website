about

Web Developer, Localization QA Tester

My name is Malgorzata. I am a junior web developer and a Polish localization tester from Montreal, Canada. I enjoy to create things and I love watching the transformation of my ideas into real life projects. I like to think of myself as a bridge between the client and the front-end user. While keeping in mind the needs of both, I always make sure my design is appealing, user-friendly and simple.

I have a diploma of Website Creation and Design from Concordia University (CCE). My technical skills include programming languages such as JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3 and also software such as Adobe: Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator, Jira, Zendesk, WordPress and Sabre GDS. I like to improve my skills by self-learning and my next step is diving into MySQL database with PHP. I also have multiple professional and personal interests and I am glad to see when they contribute to the development of my projects.
